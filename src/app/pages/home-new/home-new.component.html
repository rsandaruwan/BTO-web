Our concept

THE TALE OF BACK TO ORIGINS

I survive because we survive

Due to the outbreak of Covid-19 our sustainable luxury boutique hotel, Ravana Garden has to close its operations in March 2020. Keeping in line with our sustainable values & going by principle of “I SURVIVE, BECAUSE WE SURVIVE, we opted to keep our staff employed and continue to pay their salaries, otherwise there would have been no way for them to feed their families. As the process of recovery of the tourism industry prolongs, we decide to look at alternative steps to survive, “TOGETHER”. "Back To Origins" online platform was born as a survival solution for Ravana Garden. Since human movements are restricted as a result of the epidemic, we thought it would be a great idea for us to take the truly Sri Lankan sustainable experience of Ravana Garden, to the door of people who would appreciate and value. Various Ravana Garden products ranging from authentic/ traditional spices, RTE food, natural seasoning blends etc.. will be sold under the brand name "Back To Origin."
