About Us

Ravana Garden's "Back to Origins" collection features authentic/sustainably produced Ceylon spices, spice & herbal blends, and ready-to-eat products, allowing you to enjoy the aromatic flavors and healthier options of different cuisines in the comfort of your own home.

Our products are made with natural ingredients and do not contain any artificial flavor enhancers or colors. To demonstrate our genuine commitment to preserving the earth, we've embraced sustainable products, and we've inspired others to join us on our sustainable journey, which we believe is the most effective way to protect our planet.

Despite the pandemic-related challenge, Ravana Gardens aspires to be optimistic, reminiscing "Back to Origins" and reaching out to the roots of authentic/ healthier choices. No employee was disadvantaged by the current challenging tourism industry, as even the local farmers were spiritually and physically uplifted by this initiative with a new beacon of hope, which was powered by the hotel staff and sourced mostly from direct farmers.

Leading the way in conscientious tourism, Ravana Garden is a sustainable luxury boutique hotel in Sri Lanka. Look onto luscious green paddy fields on one side and a golden beach on the other, perfect for rejuvenating the soul. With a bird sanctuary in the neighbourhood amongst other attractions, you can also indulge in succulent organic dishes that are both nutritious and delicious. Ravana Garden is so much more than a stay away. It is wholly, an experience in itself.
